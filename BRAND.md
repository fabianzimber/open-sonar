# 📡 Open Sonar: Brand Design Guide

## 1. Brand Concept: "The Radiant Ping"
Open Sonar is about clarity through sound. The design language is built on **concentricity** (waves moving outward) and **radiance** (energy and light). Unlike "flat" minimalism, "Living" minimalism uses light-source logic—as if the interface itself is glowing.

---

## 2. Living Color Palette
This palette uses high-energy "Bio-Digital" colors that feel alive on OLED screens and bright in print.

| Role | Color Name | HEX Code | Visual Energy |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Primary (The Ping)** | **Sonar Cyan** | `#00F0FF` | Electric, liquid, and high-frequency. |
| **Secondary (Active)** | **Bio-Lime** | `#CCFF00` | Organic, "living" energy. Best for "Listening" states. |
| **Accent (Warmth)** | **Electric Orchid** | `#BF5AF2` | Adds depth and prevents the brand from feeling too cold. |
| **Base (Light Mode)** | **Solar White** | `#F7F9FC` | A crisp, blue-tinted white for maximum brightness. |
| **Base (Dark Mode)** | **Deep Abyss** | `#050A15` | A rich, near-black navy that makes the Cyan glow. |

---

## 3. The Logo: "The Open Wave"
*   **The Symbol:** Three concentric arcs of varying thickness. 
    *   The innermost arc is the thickest (the source).
    *   The outermost arc is a thin hairline (the reach).
    *   The arcs are not perfect semi-circles; they are slightly "weighted" to the right to imply forward motion.
*   **The Wordmark:** `open sonar` in **Outfit** (a modern, circular geometric font).
    *   The "o" in sonar is replaced by a solid **Sonar Cyan** dot to represent the "ping."

---

## 4. Typography: Modern & Round
To maintain the "Sonar" theme, we use fonts with circular "o"s and "e"s.

*   **Primary Heading:** `Outfit` (Bold / Extra Bold)
    *   *Vibe:* Friendly, tech-forward, and very "Google-esque."
*   **Body Text:** `Plus Jakarta Sans` (Medium)
    *   *Vibe:* Designed for high-readability in modern dashboards.
*   **Micro-copy:** `JetBrains Mono`
    *   *Vibe:* Used for technical data or "Real-time" status readouts to show the Open Source/Developer roots.

---

## 5. Visual Elements & Layout
*   **Gradients:** Avoid solid fills for large buttons. Use a "Living Gradient": `Sonar Cyan` (0%) to `Electric Orchid` (100%) at a 135-degree angle.
*   **The "Glow" Principle:** Every active element should have a soft `0 0 20px` drop shadow in its own color to simulate light emission.
*   **Corners:** Extremely rounded (Corner radius: `24px` or `Full Capsule`). Sharp corners are forbidden.

## 7. Real-time Speech Interaction States (UI)
*   **Idle:** A single, slow-pulsing **Sonar Cyan** dot.
*   **Listening:** The dot expands into a fluid, "liquid" ring of **Bio-Lime**.
*   **Thinking:** Three dots orbiting each other in a circular "sonar scan" animation.
*   **Speaking:** A vibrant waveform that transitions from **Cyan to Orchid** based on the volume of the voice.
